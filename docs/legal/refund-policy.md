# SecondBrainWare Refund Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-13. Operator: Howard Jachter. Support: support@2ndbrainware.com._

We want you to be happy with SecondBrainWare. If something isn't working out, here is exactly how refunds work.

## 14-Day No-Questions-Asked Window

New paying subscribers (Pro or Power) can request a full refund within **14 days** of their first paid charge. We don't ask why. One refund per customer per lifetime under this policy — repeat sign-ups don't reset the clock.

## How to Request a Refund

Email **support@2ndbrainware.com** with the subject line "Refund request" and the email address used at checkout. Howard personally handles refunds.

- **Target SLA:** acknowledged within 1 business day, resolved within **3 business days**.
- Refunds are issued through Stripe back to the original payment method. Funds typically post within 5–10 business days depending on the card issuer.

## Annual Subscriptions — Early Cancellation

If you cancel an annual plan after the 14-day window, we refund the **unused full months** (calculated from the cancellation date, rounded down). The current month is not refunded. Example: cancel 4 months into a 12-month plan, get 8 months back.

## Tier Downgrades

Downgrades (Power → Pro, Pro → Free) take effect at the **end of the current billing period**. We do **not** issue pro-rated refunds for mid-cycle downgrades — you keep the higher tier until the period ends.

## Apple In-App Purchases (iOS)

If you subscribed through the iOS App Store, **Apple — not SecondBrainWare — controls the refund.** Request it at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com). We have no ability to refund App Store charges directly. Apple's standard refund policy applies.

## Chargebacks and Disputes

We prefer you email us first — refunds are faster than chargebacks. If you file a chargeback through your bank or card issuer, Stripe handles the dispute on our behalf. We will submit:

- Account creation and login records
- Subscription and usage timestamps
- Any prior support correspondence
- This refund policy as published

Customers who chargeback without contacting support first may have their account closed.

## BYOK API Keys

The BYOK model (LIVE BOOKS) means you pay your AI provider directly. We never see those charges and cannot refund them. Direct provider billing disputes to Anthropic, OpenAI, or whichever provider you've connected.

## Service Downtime

We do not currently offer an uptime SLA or downtime credits. A Pro-tier uptime guarantee is on the roadmap; this policy will be updated when it ships.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be emailed to active subscribers at least 14 days before taking effect. Existing subscriptions are honored under the policy in force at the time of purchase.
