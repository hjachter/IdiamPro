# Contact Us

**We'd love to hear from you.**

Whether you've hit a snag, have an idea to make IdiamPro better, or just want to tell us how you're using it — reach out. IdiamPro is built by a small, dedicated team, and real feedback from people who think for a living is what shapes the product.

## Email us

The fastest way to reach us is by email:

**support@2ndbrainware.com**

We read every message. We aim to respond within **[1–2 business days]**. To help us help you faster, please include:

- what you were trying to do,
- what happened instead,
- which platform you're on (Mac, iPhone/iPad, or web browser), and
- a screenshot, if it's a visual issue.

## What we can help with

- **Getting started** — setup, importing your notes, and finding your way around.
- **Backups and your data** — how to keep your outlines safe and backed up. (Your data lives on your own device or browser, so backups matter — we're glad to walk you through it.)
- **AI features** — how they work, what gets sent where, and bringing your own AI key.
- **Billing and plans** — questions about your trial, Pro subscription, or a charge.
- **Bugs and feature requests** — tell us what's broken or what you wish it did.
- **Partnerships and press** — we're open to conversations; email us with the details.
