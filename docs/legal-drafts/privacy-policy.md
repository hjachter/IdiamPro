# IdiamPro — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [DATE — set on publish]**

This Privacy Policy explains how **SecondBrainWare LLC** ("SecondBrainWare," "we," "us," or "our"), a Washington limited liability company, handles information in connection with the **IdiamPro** application and website (the "Service").

**Summary in plain language:** IdiamPro is local-first. Your outlines and documents live on your own device (or in your browser for the web version), not on our servers. We do not sell your data and we do not use your content to train AI models. When you choose to use an AI feature, the specific content you submit is sent to a third-party AI provider to generate a result — and only then.

---

## 1. Overview

IdiamPro is a professional, AI-native outlining tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Your outlines and documents are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers for storage. This policy describes what limited data is processed when you use the Service, and especially when you use AI-powered features.

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not maintain a server-side database of your outlines or documents.
- We do not track your in-app behavior to build advertising profiles.
- We do not sell, rent, or monetize your personal information.
- We do not use your content to train AI models.

We do not receive or store this content on our servers except transiently when you invoke a feature that requires processing (see AI features below).

**e) Audio (for transcription).** If you use audio recording/transcription, audio is recorded locally and sent to a transcription provider only when you choose to transcribe it.

## 4. Third-Party AI and Service Providers

When you use AI-powered features, your content is sent to third-party services for processing. This only occurs when you explicitly invoke an AI feature.

- **Google Gemini (Google).** Used for AI text generation, content expansion, outline creation/transformation, source extraction, and the help chat. **Data sent:** the outline text, node names, and prompts relevant to the specific operation you initiated. **Retention:** Google processes the data to generate a response per its API terms.
- **OpenAI.** Used for podcast audio synthesis (text-to-speech), and may be used for other premium AI features. **Data sent:** the generated script or prompt text for the specific operation. **Retention:** per OpenAI's API terms.
- **AssemblyAI.** Used for audio transcription with speaker detection. **Data sent:** the audio recording you choose to transcribe. **Retention:** processed per AssemblyAI's API terms; recordings are not stored permanently.

**Bring Your Own Key (BYOK).** If you provide your own AI provider key, requests go to that provider under your own account, and that provider's privacy policy and terms govern that processing. In that case we do not see the content of those requests.

Other processors that support the Service may include our hosting provider (**[Vercel]**), identity provider, payment processor, and crash-reporting provider. We recommend signing appropriate Data Processing Agreements with each. **[HUMAN/LEGAL: execute DPAs with Google, hosting, identity, payment, and crash-reporting providers as applicable.]**

## 5. How We Use Information

We use the limited information we process to: provide and operate the Service and the features you request; process AI operations you initiate; manage accounts, trials, and payments; keep the Service secure and reliable (including diagnosing crashes); comply with legal obligations; and communicate with you about the Service (such as support and important notices).

## 6. Data Storage, Backup, and Your Responsibility

Because IdiamPro is local-first, **you are responsible for the security, storage, and backup of your data.** We strongly recommend keeping your IdiamPro files in an automatically-backed-up location (iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, or an external Time Machine disk).

**Web (browser) users:** your outlines are stored in your browser's local storage on that one device and browser. Your data can be lost if you clear your browser cache/history/site data, use private/incognito mode, or if the browser evicts stored data. Export your outlines regularly to a backed-up location.

To the maximum extent permitted by law, we disclaim liability for loss, corruption, or unavailability of data stored on your device or in your browser. See the Terms of Service for the full disclaimer.

## 7. Microphone Access

IdiamPro requests microphone access only when you use audio recording for transcription. Audio is recorded locally and sent to the transcription provider only when you explicitly choose to transcribe it. You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device settings.

You can decline and continue using all non-AI features. You can revoke consent at any time in Settings, which disables AI features until you grant consent again.
